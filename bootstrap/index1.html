Coupon-X : The one stop destination for coupons


Electronic Commerce is process of doing business through computer networks. A person sitting on his chair in front of a computer can access all the facilities of the Internet to buy or sell the products. The consumer moves through the internet to the merchant's web site.

From there, he decides that he wants to purchase something, so he is moved to the online transaction server, where all of the information he gives is encrypted along with payment section.

This website provides an additional feature which deals with exchange of digital coupons. When some digital coupons are not required by the customer/user then those coupons can be shared on the website, any other person if interested can buy those coupons for an affordable price.

Consider a scenario where a user involves in a transaction in Phone-pe app , Purchasing some goods in an online market or even some companies issue some coupons for marketing. Assume the user has received a Lens-Kart coupon(giving 20%off on the titan frame).This coupon might not be useful to the user as the user doesn‚Äôt use spectacles. This leads to inefficient usage of coupons. To avoid this , there might be a person in need of those coupons so the user can exchange his/her coupon with others.

The procedure involved in Coupon-eXchange is followed as The user logs in into his account and order the products online based on his Interest. During this process the user might receive some coupons provided by the companies. Some of these coupons may not be useful to the user. The user can then upload or share his coupons online using Coupon-X website. The price for coupons can also be assigned according to the user but in a reasonable manner. Another user who is need of the coupon can search for the required coupon and purchase them. In this way , both users are benefitted . The Database stores the records of the user accounts and transactions etc.
